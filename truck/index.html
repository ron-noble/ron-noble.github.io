About Us

Our company brand EEGROUP, part of Eastern European Export and Eastern European Haulage, holds and maintains expansive knowledge in various sectors of transportation from Bulk Liquid Haulage to Dry Bulk Powder Transport Haulage. We are acknowledged as the UK’s most trusted exporter of Used Tanker Trailers to Eastern Europe, supplying the majority of industrial sectors from Food and Chemical through to Construction and the Oil industry.

With the company being born in the early 1990’s, it’s given us 30 years to become the primary Commercial Trailer Exporter from the UK and Northern Ireland to Eastern Europe. In this time we have geographically expanded our business with offices across Eastern Europe, St. Petersburg, Lithuania, Klaipeda to Belarus (Minsk) and Ukraine (lviv).

EExport & EEhaulage are an independent family run business that developed with our Eastern European heritage. The core to our export business is upheld by our long term relationships with UK hauliers, logistics companies, trailer rental companies, and commercial asset finance companies. With us being a family run business, we provide an organic and personal service putting our customers first to make sure they’re 100% satisfied with the service overall as our regular returning customers are the upholstery and main asset to our business. This is where our export business comes into play. We offer the solution of permanent export with proof of bill of Laiding so our customers can be assured their Used Road Tankers won't be resold on the UK or Irish markets to competitors. We will provide correct shipping documents and can provide our shipping agents' contact details to give UK sellers the peace of mind.
